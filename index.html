About Sam Leaks

Sam Leaks is a curated collection of uncopylocked Roblox games. Our mission is to provide a high-quality, easy-to-navigate platform for the Roblox development community.

What We Offer

We provide access to a wide variety of uncopylocked Roblox games that you can download, study, and modify for your own projects. Our collection is constantly updated with new content.

How to Use Uncopylocked Games

Step 1: Download

Click on any game card and hit the download button to get the .rblx file.

Step 2: Open in Studio

Open Roblox Studio and go to File > Open from File, then select your downloaded file.

Step 3: Customize

Modify scripts, assets, and gameplay to make the game your own!

Step 4: Publish

Once you're done, publish your customized game to Roblox!

Tips for Developers

Learn from Code

Study the scripts to understand game mechanics and scripting patterns used by other developers.

Customize & Improve

Don't just copy - add your own features, fix bugs, and make improvements to create something unique.

Give Credit

If you use significant portions of code or assets, consider crediting the original creators.

Test Thoroughly

Always test uncopylocked games for exploits or backdoors before publishing to ensure player safety.
